ABOUT VISHAL's REGENCY



Vishal's Regency Group of Hotels had its maiden foray into the hospitality industry in 1982 by way of a small hotel at Rourkela.
Subsequently, eleven boutique and one state-of-the art convention properties were set up at Puri, Darjeeling, Bhubaneswar, Gangtok,
Goa, Gopalpur, Kalimpong and Raipur.



The Vishal's Regency Group with its successful chain of eleven luxury boutique hotels and resorts has been catering to the requirement
of todays business and leisure traveler. Its pan- Indian presence has a network of signature hotels and Resorts in scenic sea sides, misty
hills & mountains and vibrant urban settings.



‘Vishal's Regency’ has become synonymous of a brand that promises guests, ultimate luxury, in the lap of nature. ‘Stay with us , Stay
with nature’, being the motto, its commitment to Eco friendly practices has never been compromised and is implemented in all its properties
in the form of rainwater harvesting, Organic farming and recycling and reusing of products.



With 5 star deluxe properties at Bhubaneswar (Vishal's Regency Lagoon) and Gangtok (Vishal's Regency Spa Resort & Casino) of the group prides
itself on having set up the first 5 star deluxe hotel in North East India and the first on-shore- live Casino in the Country.



A consummate, committed team of very courteous staff delivers services that has few equals, where the guests’ every predilection is
noted and gratified with utmost priority and personal privacy, is given utmost respect.
